About Eden Hotel and Resort

EDEN HOTEL AND RESORTS

Is truly residential, like a home away from home, with our guests at the comfort of their individual rooms, having access to our pool, lounge and shaded pavilions. For this reason, it appeals to those with specific wellness goals in mind, and it's suitable for corporate organizations, couples on honeymoon, individuals, travelers, groups and families. A stay at EDEN Hotel and Resorts offers more than just a holiday, It is also an opportunities to experience our world of comfort and luxury.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission is to ensure that every guest who visits, stays or dines with us is completely satisfied and happy with every aspect of Eden Hotel because, everything counts.

GYM AND SPA

If you are looking to jumpstart a new fitness regime, or to simply enhance your current healthy lifestyle, then Eden Gym & Spa is designed for you. it is fitted with modern state of the art equipments, highly trained and seasoned staff poised to deliver top-notch wellness programmes to our discerning clients.

ACCOMMODATION

SUITES: Eden Suites opens up to their own balconies and outdoor areas, allowing panoramic view of the beautiful countryside.

ROOMS Our Rooms are well lit with exquisite furniture and adorned with beautiful paintings. Each room opens onto a balcony offering great views of the otta landscape fitted with modern facilities.

Our room comes in different categories to meet the specific needs of our guests.

RESTAURANT AND DINING

Enjoy the flavor of different cuisines from all over the world with professional guidance on nutrition requirements and sophisticated tastes created by our seasoned chefs using organic ingredients that are sourced locally. We offer all day set menu revealing the potency of certain meals to maximize personal preferences and ensure absolute satisfaction in line with healthy consumption lifestyle. Our fine dining experience offers a variety of options to suit different moods and tastes, from a romantic dinner for two to a family meal.

SWIMMING POOL

Our 7m by 14m sized pool with a depth of 4ft to 8ft located at the boat house with a natural lay out provides the perfect atmosphere for relaxation, exercise, and interactions.

The pool is professionally maintained on a daily basis to ensure a sparkling effect. It also features indoor games such as table tennis, snooker, chess, scrabble and more.
